Midas Touch

"Semester Project 2"

HTML

SASS

JS

BOOTSTRAP

This is my Semester Project 2, where I applied the skills I’ve developed over the past three semesters to create an auction website. The project involves building a platform where users can list items for auction and place bids on items posted by others. Upon registration, new users receive 1,000 credits to participate in the bidding process. Users can earn more credits by selling items and spend credits by purchasing them. While non-registered users can browse the listings, only registered users are allowed to place bids.

☙ About me ❧

My name is Rena Hashimi, a 34-year-old living in Oslo. I’m married, a mother to two wonderful children, and work full-time in the field of optics. Alongside these responsibilities, I am also pursuing my passion for technology by studying to become a Frontend Developer at the Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media, having just completed my first year.

Technology, coding, web development, and design have always been my passions. After spending nearly 16 years in customer service, I decided it was time for a career shift and took the leap to follow my dream of becoming a developer.

My first year at Noroff was incredibly rewarding, and now in my fourth semester, I’m even more excited for the opportunity to continue deepening my knowledge and growing as a developer.
